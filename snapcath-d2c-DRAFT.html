Life with a line is hard enough

You shouldn't have to plan your day around your line.

Taping up before every shower. Worrying the site will snag on a sleeve or the sheets. Layers of dressings that tug at your skin. For the weeks or months you're living with a PICC line or catheter, the little things add up.

SnapCath™ was designed by an ICU nurse who watched patients struggle with exactly this — and thought line care should feel a whole lot simpler.

"Can I shower today?"

Plastic wrap and tape shouldn't be part of your morning routine.

"Please don't snag."

Getting dressed and sleeping shouldn't feel like a risk.

"My skin is raw."

Constant adhesive and re-taping takes a toll over weeks of wear.

How it works

Three steps. No taping ritual.

1

Place it

Position SnapCath™ over your line and site — a single piece, no layering.

2

Snap it shut

Close the lid to secure and protect the site. That's your everyday cover.

3

Open when needed

Pop the lid for site checks or dressing changes — no full removal.
