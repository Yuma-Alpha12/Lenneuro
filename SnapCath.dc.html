Problems & Frustrations

Securing a catheter today means layering multiple single-purpose products.

None of them designed to work together.

Problems
  • ●Clear film dressings fail and lift repeatedly
  • ●Anchors hold the line but leave the catheter site exposed
  • ●Lines are dislodged during dressing changes
  • ●Multiple products required at every weekly change
  • ●Requires an RN or clinic visit for every dressing change
Frustrations
  • ●Pain and skin tearing with every peel-and-replace change
  • ●No shower-proof dressings — limited bathing leads to poor hygiene and secondary infections
  • ●Current dressings aren't tamper resistant
  • ●Lost independence — patients can't manage care themselves
