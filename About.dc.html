About Lenneuro

A medtech company built by request of clinicians, patients and providers

Lenneuro is developing SnapCath™, an external catheter securement accessory designed to support catheter securement and site coverage, founded by a clinician who saw the challenges of line care firsthand.

Why we started

Our founder saw the same problem repeat, shift after shift.

Our founder, Abbey Lenore Azar, spent years at the bedside in the ICU and emergency department, as well as in legal nurse consulting. Through that experience, she saw the practical challenges that patients, caregivers, and healthcare professionals can face when managing catheter securement and site coverage.

Existing approaches often address different aspects of line care through separate products. Lenneuro was founded to explore a more integrated and user-centered approach.

Our mission

Give every patient with a vascular access line their life back.
