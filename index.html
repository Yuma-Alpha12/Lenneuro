Lenneuro is developing SnapCath™, an external catheter securement accessory designed to support catheter securement and site coverage.

SnapCath™ is currently under development. It has not been cleared or approved by the U.S. Food and Drug Administration and is not available for sale or commercial distribution.

In Development

An integrated approach to catheter securement and site coverage

An integrated design

Designed to support catheter securement and site coverage in a single accessory.

Designed with skin in mind

A design goal of the device is to limit adhesive contact with the skin during wear.

Site access by design

Features an openable lid intended to allow routine site access without removing the adhesive.

Designed to be water resistant

Water resistance for everyday wear is a design objective and is being evaluated in development.

The Lenneuro device is currently under development. It has not been cleared or approved by the U.S. Food and Drug Administration and is not available for sale or commercial distribution. Product images show design prototypes, final product appearance and specifications may differ.
